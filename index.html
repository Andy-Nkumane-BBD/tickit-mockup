How It Works

  1. 1

    Create a Project

    Set up your project with a title, description, and deadline. Customize your Kanban columns to match your workflow.

  2. 2

    Add Team Members

    Invite collaborators and assign roles: Admin (full control), Collaborator (can edit), or Viewer (read-only access).

  3. 3

    Create Tasks

    Add tasks with due dates, priority levels (Low, Medium, High, Urgent), and custom labels for organization.

  4. 4

    Track Progress

    Move tasks across your Kanban board as they progress. Receive notifications for updates and approaching deadlines.
